How we calculate typing speed ?

This is a simple and standard method of calculating the typing speed. All the words you type in a minute is your typing speed. typing speed generally calculate in WPM.

What is WPM ?

WPM stands for WORDS PER MINUTE. WPM is a worldwide standard to calculate the typing speed.

What is CPM ?

CPM stands for CHARACTERS PER MINUTE. The realtion between CPM and WPM is

CPM = 5 * WPM


What is the size of a word ?

The standard size of a word is 5 keystrokes.

What is a keystroke?

Every key you press on the keyboard to type a letter/character is one keystroke.

What is typing ?

Typing is the process of writing or inputting text by pressing keys on a typewriter, computer keyboard, cell phone, or calculator.

How to type ?

Typing is all about the idea that each finger has its own area on the keyboard. Every Fingers of both hands have their specific keys. We press every key with their specific finger only. By praciticing regularly fingers will learn their locations and keys in muscle memory, then we can type seamless without looking at the keyboard.

Can I learn typing in 10 days ?

Typing can not be learned in some days. It is ridiculous if i say you can learn typing in 10 days. It requires patience. You don't need to type whole day. 15 - 30 minutes everyday is enough, After some time you can adjust according to your comfort.

Sitting Posture for typing -

This is the first thing, you have to consider. Believe me if you don't sit properly you may hurt your back.


Good sitting posture when type -
  • Rest your back against the chair for maximum support.
  • Rest your feet flat on the floor or on a footrest.
  • Look straight ahead without leaning forward.
  • Position your knees at the same level or slightly lower than your hips
  • Relax your shoulders.
  • Keep at least 45 - 70 cm of distance between your eyes and the screen.
  • Еxpose the shoulder, arm, and wrist muscles to the least possible strain.
  • The wrists can touch the tabletop in front of the keyboard. Never shift your body weight to the wrists by resting on them.

We can categorize in three categories based on there typing speeds -

Beginner

  • Typing speed below 25 WPM
  • Look at keyboard while typing
  • Difficulty in pressig some tricky keys
  • Diffuculty in captial letters
  • Use Caps-Lock instead of Shift
  • Can't type special symbols
  • Don't know key locations

Intermediate

  • Typing speed between 25 - 45 WPM
  • Don't look at keyboard while typing
  • Difficulty in pressig some tricky keys
  • Comfortable in captial letters
  • Use Shift instead of Caps-Lock
  • Can type some special symbols
  • know most key locations
  • Frequently uses Back Space

Advance

  • Typing speed above 45 WPM
  • Don't look at keyboard while typing
  • Can press keys
  • Comfortable in captial letters
  • Use Shift instead of Caps-Lock
  • Can type every special symbols
  • know every key locations
